Meet our Team

Our highly qualified committed team develop tailormade oral health program for every child patient to help them achieve optimum oral health making dental visit a positive experience. As your oral health care providers, we are committed to continual education and learning.  We attend dental workshops, dental conferences and conventions to stay informed of new techniques, the latest products, and the newest equipment that a modern dental office can utilize to provide state of art dental care.  Also, being members of various professional dental associations helps us to stay well informed with the changes and recommendations for our profession. Our values are at the core of every decision we make at "White" a unit of TOHcc - Total Oral Health Care Center.
